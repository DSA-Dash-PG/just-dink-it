How it works

A circuit, a standing, and a story.

The Dink Society runs in Circuits — each one a seven-week social league with real competition, real standings, and a drink afterward. Here's how a Circuit runs.

01 · Format

Round-robin into a gold-medal final.

Each Circuit runs six teams per division over seven weeks. Divisions play on the same nights under one Circuit umbrella, so whether you're in 3.0 Mixed or 3.5 Mixed, you're part of the same season. Six weeks of round-robin, then championship week. Every match feeds your division standings, your stats, and your Circuit Points.

02 · A night at the Society

Two rounds. Twelve games. Four points on the line.

Each league night is one match played across two rounds, with six games per round — one women's doubles, one men's doubles, and four mixed doubles. Games go to 11, win by 1. Whoever wins the most games takes the round; ties split it. Four round points up for grabs, and every match feeds your division standings.

  • :00
    Arrive & exchange lineups

    Captains trade written lineups ten minutes before play. Confirm court assignments, get loose.

  • :15
    Round 1 · Six games

    Home serves first, away chooses side. Women's doubles, men's doubles, then four mixed games. Most games won wins the round — 2 points for the win, 1 for a tie.

  • 1:00
    Round 2 · Six games

    Sides flip. Home chooses side, away serves first. Same lineup structure — one women's, one men's, four mixed. Another 2 points on the line.

  • 1:45
    Sign the scoresheet & stay

    Both captains sign off — either on paper or electronically. Scores go straight into the Circuit leaderboard. Then it's the after-court round, which is where the Society actually lives.

03 · Circuit points

Your finish follows you across Circuits.

Circuit Points are the Society's long game. Each Circuit, final placement banks points toward your all-time standing — and weekly bonuses (sweeps, perfect attendance, standout performances) stack on top. One Circuit is a season. The Circuit leaderboard is your legacy.
