Society Circuit · Year-end prize

Every league counts toward one cup.

Players earn Society Circuit points across every league they play in. Top of the Circuit at year-end takes the Society Champion title — paddle, cash, and bragging rights for a year.

Team Rosters

  • League fee is due at registration to confirm your team's spot.
  • Final rosters due one week prior to the first game.
  • Players must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Captains are responsible for collecting waivers, rosters, and payment.
  • Minimum 4 players per match; max 8 per day.
  • Maximum 12 rostered players per team.
  • Only rostered players may compete.

League Format

  • Doubles round-robin format.
  • Each match has rounds in mixed formats: WD, MD, MXD.
  • Five round-robin weeks → one rivalry week → one championship week.
  • Top 2 teams play for gold at Week 7.
  • Weekly standings updates after every match.

Match Day Rules

  • Check in 30 minutes prior to start.
  • Exchange lineups 10 minutes prior to play.
  • Games to 11, win by 1.
  • 10-minute grace period for the first game.
  • Rotate partners each game — only play with same partner once per round.
  • Max 3 games per player.
  • Non-rostered player = automatic forfeit.

Match Scoring

  • 2 points = win · 1 point = tie · 0 points = loss.
  • Max 4 match points per match (2 rounds × 2 max).
  • Top 4 teams advance to playoffs after Week 6.
  • Tiebreakers: PTS → GW → PS → DIFF.
  • (Match points → Games won → Points scored → Differential.)

Officiating

  • Self-regulated games.
  • Sideline players may NOT make calls or coach during play.
  • Kitchen line foot faults: 1st call = warning; 2nd = point lost.
  • Scores submitted by both captains. If they disagree, the league reviews.

Etiquette

  • Fun, friendly competition for those that love the game.
  • Swearing, negative taunting, or poor sportsmanship is not tolerated.
  • Cheer good play — yours or theirs.
  • Stay after if you can. The post-match hang is the league.
The Composite Score

How we rank players individually.

The Composite is a 0–100 score that balances winning consistently, dominating when you do, and showing up. It's the default sort on the Leaderboard's League tab.

Composite = (Win % × 40) + (Avg margin ÷ 2) + (Games won × 1.5)

Example: 10–2 record (.833), +42 differential across 12 games, 18 games won. Composite = (.833 × 40) + (3.5 × 24 ÷ 12 ÷ 2) + (18 × 1.5) ≈ 92.
