Waiver & release of liability.

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective: Summer 2026 season
Draft notice: This is a starter waiver template for The Dink Society. Before going live, have a California-licensed attorney review for compliance with state law, your insurance policy, and facility requirements.

1. Acknowledgment of risk

I understand that participation in The Dink Society pickleball league involves inherent risks of physical injury, including but not limited to sprains, strains, fractures, head injuries, and other harm caused by collisions, falls, equipment, weather conditions, court surfaces, or the actions of other players or spectators. I voluntarily assume all such risks.

2. Release of liability

In consideration of being permitted to participate, I, on behalf of myself and my heirs, release and discharge The Dink Society, its organizers, sponsors, captains, host facilities, and their respective officers, employees, and agents (collectively, the "Released Parties") from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action arising out of my participation, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

3. Medical authorization

I represent that I am physically able to participate and have no medical condition that would prevent safe participation. I authorize the Released Parties to obtain emergency medical care on my behalf if I am unable to do so, and I assume responsibility for all related costs.

4. Photo and media release

I grant The Dink Society permission to photograph, record, and use my name, likeness, image, and voice in connection with the league for promotional, editorial, and historical purposes, without compensation, in any media now known or later developed. I may opt out by emailing hi@thedinksociety.com.

5. Code of conduct

I agree to abide by the Player Code of Conduct, the published League Rules, and any reasonable instructions from league organizers or facility staff. I understand that violations may result in removal from a match, suspension, or expulsion from the league without refund.

6. No refund of fees

I understand that league fees are non-refundable after the published cutoff date. Weather cancellations, facility closures, personal injury, or schedule conflicts do not entitle me to a refund.

7. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California. Any dispute will be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Los Angeles County, California.

8. Signature

By checking the acknowledgment box during registration, or by clicking the link sent to your email and confirming, I confirm that I have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Waiver & Release of Liability. I am 18 years of age or older.

Questions about this waiver? Email hi@thedinksociety.com.
